Key Narrative

Riders love the smoothness and increasingly trust the technology, but wait times remain the #1 pain point, dragging overall sentiment. Coverage limitations and pricing concerns are secondary friction points. Trust has grown steadily over 12 months, with positive sentiment reaching 65%+ by Q1 2026. The holiday pricing controversy in December 2025 caused a measurable dip, but recovery was swift.

Methodology

Data Source: Synthetic dataset modeled on publicly reported Waymo rider sentiment patterns from Google Play Store reviews. 3,075 reviews spanning April 2025 through March 2026 across 6 cities.

Sentiment Analysis: Custom VADER-style lexicon-based sentiment analyzer with compound scoring [-1, +1]. Handles negation, booster words, and punctuation emphasis. Classification thresholds: positive (≥ 0.05), negative (≤ -0.05), neutral (between).

Topic Extraction: Keyword-based topic classification across 7 categories (Ride Quality, Wait Times, Safety/Trust, App/UX, Coverage, Pricing, General). Multi-topic assignment supported with primary topic designation.

Event Study: 30-day pre/post windows around publicly announced Waymo milestones. Measures average compound sentiment shift to identify causal impact signals.
